Some things about Me

My name is Dario Richter. I am 14 years old. I'm currently in the 9th grade at the Adolf-Pichler-Platz high school. I live with my family in Zirl. I have always been interested in computers in general and wanted to understand how they work. One day I bought a book about Java even though I don't really like reading books very much. Through this book I found my enthusiasm for the programming world. I love the ever new challenges and the feeling of having mastered them. The joy of programming is what makes me happy and my goal is to get better and better at it.
